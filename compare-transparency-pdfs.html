Analysis

Look for these indicators of correct transparency handling:

  • ✓ Transparent areas should show the checkerboard pattern through them
  • ✓ Logo colors should remain vibrant and accurate
  • ✓ No white boxes around logos where transparency should be
  • ✗ Inverted transparency (colored areas transparent, transparent areas opaque)
  • ✗ All black or missing content
